CheeseBurger and French Fries

A cheeseburger is a hamburger with cheese added. Hamburgers are sandwiches typically made with ground meat, usually beef, in a bun or on a bread roll. French fries are thin, usually deep-fried strips of potato.

Nachos

It consists of tortilla chips layered with melted cheese, bacon, and possibly other toppings. Nachos are a type of appetizer or snack originating in Mexican cuisine. They are typically made with corn tortilla chips, melted cheese, and jalapenos. Other common toppings include beans, salsa, sour cream, guacamole, and ground beef.

Pizza

It is a savory dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, pineapple, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

French Fries With Ketchup And Mayonnaise.

French Fries are a type of potato that is cut into strips and then fried in oil. French fries are a popular side dish and snack food. They are often served with ketchup, mayonnaise, or other dipping sauces.

Lamb Chops

Lamb Chops are slices of meat cut from the rib section of a lamb. Lamb chops are a popular dish that can be grilled, baked, or pan-fried. They are often seasoned with herbs and spices before cooking.

Shish Kebabs

They are a type of skewered and grilled meat dish originating in Middle Eastern cuisine . Shish kebabs are typically made from cubed or ground meat, marinated and cooked on skewers over charcoal or open flame. The type of meat used can vary, including lamb, beef, chicken or even seafood.
